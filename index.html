Hello :) I'm Nana Sanz

When I discovered the art of ecoprinting, I was so amazed with the idea that I could print using plants; I just had to spend some time walking through nature, identifying leaves, foraging, and playing with them to create beautiful compositions.

In this inspirational path, I was getting everytime more concieuss and respectful for every single living being on this planet.

Botanical printing has become a very important part of my journey, as much as spredding the word for slow living and councious choice making.
